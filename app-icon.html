How to Use as App Icon:

  1. Take a screenshot of your preferred design (Cmd+Shift+4 on Mac)
  2. Open the screenshot in Preview
  3. Use the selection tool to select just the icon (hold Shift for perfect square)
  4. Copy (Cmd+C) and create new from clipboard (Cmd+N)
  5. Export as PNG at 1024x1024 pixels
  6. In Xcode, go to Assets.xcassets
  7. Drag your PNG to the AppIcon slots
  8. Xcode will automatically generate all required sizes

Alternative: Use an online tool like appicon.co to generate all sizes from your 1024x1024 image.
